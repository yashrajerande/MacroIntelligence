# Scoring Framework — Indian Conglomerates Strategic Intelligence
This is the canonical scoring rubric for the ConglomeratesTracker department.
StrategyAdvisor consumes it. CriticReviewer enforces it. Do not reinterpret.

## Core Scoring Framework (1-10)
For each conglomerate, score on:

1. **Vision at Scale** — ambition to build large, category-defining
   businesses; entry into new sectors; platform thinking.
2. **Talent Bench Strength** — leadership depth; ability to attract
   operators; succession quality.
3. **Speed of Execution** — pace from strategy → delivery; ability to
   scale projects.
4. **Ethical Reputation & Trust** — governance quality; regulatory comfort;
   public credibility.
5. **Access & Influence** — government relationships; regulatory leverage;
   ecosystem power.
6. **Strategic Assets / Edge** — free cash flow engines; dominant
   platforms; partnerships; unique capabilities.
7. **Capital Deployment Aggression** — capex scale; acquisitions;
   fundraising; risk appetite.

Each dimension MUST include a delta vs the previous cycle (+1, 0, -1, etc.).

## Overlay 1 — Strategic Power Map (1-10)
Political / regulatory influence · Capital market power · Family control
stability · Global expansion capability · AI / energy-transition positioning.

## Overlay 2 — Debt Wall / Refinancing Risk (0-10)
Maturity profile · Refinancing dependence · Cost of capital access.
Higher score = higher risk.

## Overlay 3 — Execution Receipts (0-10)
Announced vs delivered · Capex actually deployed · Project completion track
record. Higher score = better delivery.

## Overlay 4 — Momentum Score (-5 to +5)
Drivers: capital deployment changes · execution progress · new sector
entry/exit · investor sentiment · leadership changes.

## Overlay 5 — Future Dominance Index (0-10)
Factors: AI positioning · energy transition assets · digital platforms ·
manufacturing depth · capital compounding ability · talent density.

## Overlay 6 — Conglomerate Control Map (1-10)
Promoter control strength · Succession stability · Board strength · Global
partners · Political connectivity.

## Tiers
- **Tier 1 — System Dominators**
- **Tier 2 — Strategic Challengers**
- **Tier 3 — Stable Compounders**
- **Tier 4 — Fragile / Declining**

## Typology Buckets
Platform Empires · Institutional Builders · Industrial Scalers · Capital
Allocators · Southern Compounders · Fragile / Leveraged.

## Tone Discipline
- No corporate language. No vague statements. No repetition.
- Prioritise insight over completeness.
- Examples of acceptable register:
  > "This group is over-ambitious relative to its balance sheet."
  > "Execution credibility is deteriorating."
  > "Capital allocation discipline is improving."

## Critical Rule
If there is no real development for a group this cycle, write: "No material
movement." Do NOT fabricate a move to fill the table.

## Output Sections (every cycle, in this order)
1. Major Strategic Moves (last 30-60 days) — table of material developments only
2. Strategic Power Dashboard — 7-dimension table with delta vs prior cycle
3. Strategic Power Map — overlay 1 table
4. Debt Wall / Fragility Overlay — overlay 2 table
5. Execution Receipts — overlay 3 table
6. Momentum Score — overlay 4 table
7. Future Dominance Index — overlay 5 table
8. Control Map — overlay 6 table
9. Ranking — Tier 1-4 placement
10. Typology — classification by bucket
11. Red Flags — explicit governance/leverage/execution gaps
12. Emerging Themes — 3-5 structural shifts
13. Bottom-Line View — 5-7 bullets on power redistribution
