India Macro Regime

Global Macro Regime

US GDP at 0.7% SAAR — down from 4.4% prior — while S&P 500 prints at the 100th percentile all-time high is the most expensive growth deceleration in a decade; the market is pricing a soft landing that the output data does not yet confirm. US core PCE ticked up to 3.1% from 3.0%, compressing the Fed's room to cut even as growth stalls — the stagflation corridor is narrow but the door is open. China GDP at 4.5% with PMI Composite at 51.5 and CPI recovering to 1.3% from 0.2% suggests the deflationary export pressure that suppressed global goods prices in 2024-25 is fading — a tailwind for India's WPI that is quietly reversing. Iron ore's single-session jump to $161.91 from $133.82 is the sharpest commodity signal in today's data: if Chinese construction demand is genuinely recovering, Brent's stickiness at $101.68 becomes a structural feature, not a spike.

INR at 94.34 (87th percentile) with FII outflows accelerating means RBI is spending reserves to defend a level — a defence that is arithmetically finite.

Equity + Real Estate Implications

Bank Nifty at 56,264 (93rd percentile, all-time high) while CD ratio sits at 83.04% and NBFC credit growth decelerates to 13% from 17% is a valuation that prices in RBI support it may not receive — the last time this divergence sustained, macro-prudential tightening via risk weights followed within two quarters. Nifty at 24,093 (77th percentile) with India VIX falling to 18.38 (14th percentile) suggests the equity market is pricing out tail risk precisely when the FII reversal and oil at $101 argue for a wider risk premium. Real estate presents a cleaner picture: home loan rates down to 8% from 9.1%, affordability index at 0.61, and retail mall vacancy at a cycle low of 5.7% — residential demand is structurally supported, but unsold inventory at 6,00,000 units and office absorption at the 24th percentile cap the upside for commercial and developer-side plays. The residential-commercial divergence in real estate mirrors the goods-services divergence in consumption — the same consumer, making the same trade-down.

Watch Wednesday's RBI liquidity operation size — if the central bank injects above ₹1.5 lakh cr, it is signalling deposit stress is acute enough to override its inflation caution, and the rate narrative changes overnight.
