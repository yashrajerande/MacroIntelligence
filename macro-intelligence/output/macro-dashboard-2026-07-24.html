Nifty 50 at 23,870 (76th percentile) with India VIX at 13.5 (8th percentile) implies the equity market is pricing near-zero tail risk at a moment when the three largest risks — oil at $100.54, US 10Y at 4.67%, and NBFC funding stress — are all simultaneously elevated. Watch the RBI's weekly deposit and credit data release: if the CD ratio crosses 84%, the probability of a macro-prudential circular on unsecured lending risk weights moves from watch to base case.
